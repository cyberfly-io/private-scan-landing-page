Privacy Policy

Last Updated

December 26, 2025

1. Zero-Knowledge Architecture

At Private Scan, we believe your data belongs to you. This application operates with a "Zero-Knowledge" architecture. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, documents, or images processed by the application.

2. Local-Only Processing

All OCR (Optical Character Recognition) operations are performed locally on your device using our proprietary Rust engine. Our application is designed to function entirely without an internet connection for core tasks.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs. The only internet communication occurs during optional translation downloads or payment processing handled by the Google Play Store securely.

4. Your Consent

By using Private Scan, you acknowledge that your data is stored solely on your device and you are responsible for its backup and security.
